/*
* PRE: A is an array of numbers. low and high are valid 
*      indices of A such that 0 <= low <= high < size of A
*
* POST: The subarray A[low...high] is sorted in 
*       non-descending order. 
*/
QuickSort(A, low, high):

    if low < high then do:

        pivot = partition(A, low, high)

        QuickSort(A, low, pivot - 1)
        QuickSort(A, pivot + 1, high)
